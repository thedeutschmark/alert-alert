Alert! Alert! - Third-Party Notices
===================================

Alert! Alert! is a thin native frontend that orchestrates two independent
third-party tools for the work it cannot do itself: fetching media from
URLs (yt-dlp) and decoding/encoding media (FFmpeg).

Alert! Alert! does NOT contain any video-downloading code. URL-based loading
is delegated entirely to yt-dlp, an independent open-source project that
Alert! Alert!'s authors do not develop, modify, or control. Media decoding
and encoding is delegated to FFmpeg, an independent open-source project.

Neither yt-dlp nor FFmpeg is bundled with Alert! Alert!. On first launch,
if these tools are not detected on your system, Alert! Alert! offers a
one-click download that fetches the official binaries directly from their
maintainers' release channels — the downloads are not proxied, rehosted,
or modified by Alert! Alert!.
